Comparing LSTM-Based Next-frame Prediction of Solar Corona Videos

Volga Sezen

This project aims to replicate common recurrent models such as LSTM’s and ConvLSTM’s to get an idea about sequence modeling. First results indicate the power of convolutional LSTM, and the fragility of the complex problem space.

Results

Below is a summary of the findings. ConvLSTM trained on 128 filter representation works better than a naive model repeating the last observed image 5 times across all metrics by a small margin.

Next-frame forecasting performance on the validation set, averaged over the 5-frame prediction horizon.
Model MAE ↓ PSNR (dB) ↑ SSIM ↑
LSTM 0.0231 30.08 0.8549
ConvLSTM-16 0.0625 22.60 0.7720
ConvLSTM-16 (vel.) 0.0128 34.45 0.9533
ConvLSTM-128 0.0087 38.10 0.9727
Naive 0.0097 37.11 0.9603
